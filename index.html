Model Transparency: How We Calculate Inherent Risk

Our model quantifies your risk based on the **Source Quality** and **Volume** of compromised credentials, without relying on proprietary internal data.

  • Weighted Credential Exposure (CER) Score: This score assigns high points (weights) to critical inputs: **Infostealer Logs** (Highest Weight 9), **Admin/C-Level Accounts** ($\times 10$ multiplier), and **New Unique Dumps** (High Weight 7). Lower-quality data receives a lower weight.
  • Annual Likelihood (L): The final CER Score is converted into a probability using a proprietary normalization factor and industry attack baselines.
  • Annualized Loss Expectancy (ALE): Calculated by multiplying the Likelihood ($\mathbf{L}$) by the estimated financial cost of a single breach (SLE).
